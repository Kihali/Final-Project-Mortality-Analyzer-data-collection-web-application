Child Mortality Analyzer

Reducing child mortality in Kenya is crucial for improving the nation's development and well-being. Our application aims to leverage data analysis to understand child mortality patterns and enhance healthcare interventions. High child mortality rates are a significant challenge, particularly in urban areas with healthcare access disparities.

This tool aligns with Kenya's national health goals and global efforts under SDG 3 to end preventable deaths of children under five by 2030. It provides insights into mortality patterns through a comprehensive database that integrates region-specific data, health facility capacities, and mortality statistics.
